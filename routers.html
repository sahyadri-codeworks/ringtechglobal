Network Routers

Routers are critical networking devices that connect multiple networks and manage the flow of data between them. They are typically used to connect internal LANs to external networks such as the internet or WAN.

Modern routers go beyond basic routing by offering advanced capabilities such as VPN (Virtual Private Network), SD-WAN, traffic optimization, firewall integration, load balancing, and network security features.
