Servers & Storage

Servers are high-performance computing systems designed to host applications, manage network resources, and process data, while storage solutions are responsible for storing, protecting, and managing large volumes of data.

Together, they form the backbone of IT infrastructure, supporting virtualization, cloud computing, databases, enterprise applications, and backup solutions.

Modern server and storage systems offer features like high availability, redundancy, scalability, RAID configurations, and hybrid cloud integration, ensuring data security, performance, and business continuity across industries.
